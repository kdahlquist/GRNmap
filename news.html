(2015) Parameter Estimation for Gene Regulatory Networks from Microarray Data: Cold Shock Response in Saccharomyces cerevisiae. Bulletin of Mathematical Biology. March 21, 2015

LMU Undergraduate Research Symposium

The joint LMU Bioinformatics and Biomathematics groups presented posters about GRNsight and GRNmap at the 7th Annual Loyola Marymount University Undergraduate Research Symposium on March 21, 2014.

  • Full symposium program
  • Abstract and poster by Nicole Anguiano and Anindita Varshneya entitled, "Improvements to GRNsight: a Web Application for Visualizing Models of Gene Regulatory Networks"
  • Abstract and poster by Juan Carrillo and Trixie Roque entitled, "Software Refactoring and Usability Enhancement for GRNmap, a Gene Regulatory Network Modeling Application"
  • Abstract and poster by Natalie Williams and K. Grace Johnson entitled, "Comparing the Dynamics of the Cold Shock Gene Regulatory Network in Yeast with a Random Network"
